Our Menu

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer butter masala is a rich, creamy, and mildly sweet North Indian curry featuring soft paneer cubes in a tomato-cashew sauce. Often called paneer makhani, it is characterized by generous amounts of butter and cream, creating a velvety, smooth texture. This popular dish offers a rich, mild, and mildly spiced flavor, perfect with naan or rice.

Chana Masala

Chana masala is a popular North Indian and Pakistani vegetarian curry featuring tender chickpeas (chana) simmered in a tangy, spiced tomato and onion sauce. It is known for its hearty, savory flavor—often enhanced with amchoor (dried mango powder) or pomegranate seeds for tartness—and is typically served with flatbreads like bhatura or rice.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani is a fragrant, celebratory South Asian one-pot dish featuring tender, marinated chicken layered with fluffy, long-grain basmati rice. It is characterized by aromatic spices like saffron, cardamom, and cinnamon, along with caramelized onions, fresh mint, and coriander. It is perhaps best known for its flavoury mix in your mouth.
